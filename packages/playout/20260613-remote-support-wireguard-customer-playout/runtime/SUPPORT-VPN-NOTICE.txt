Multivideo Playout bundles the WireGuard for Windows installer for dedicated remote support.

The support VPN runs as a dedicated WireGuard tunnel:
- Tunnel service: WireGuardTunnel$multivideo-support
- Configuration: C:\ProgramData\Multivideo\Playout\support-vpn
- Routes: only the Multivideo support VPN subnet, never the customer's default internet route

Bundled installer:
- support-vpn\wireguard-amd64.msi
